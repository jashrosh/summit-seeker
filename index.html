Frequently Asked Questions

What fitness level do I need for your treks?

Our treks range from beginner to advanced. Each trek listing includes specific fitness requirements. Generally, even for beginner treks, we recommend being able to walk 5-7 miles with some elevation gain.

What equipment do I need to bring?

We provide a detailed packing list after booking. We supply camping equipment, but you'll need personal items like appropriate clothing, hiking boots, and a daypack.

How large are your trekking groups?

Our groups typically range from 6-12 people to ensure quality experience and minimize environmental impact.

What's your cancellation policy?

Full refund if canceled 60+ days before departure, 50% refund if 30-59 days, no refund if less than 30 days. Trip insurance is highly recommended.
